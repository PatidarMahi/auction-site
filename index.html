[ About ]

The purpose is to help the NIT Jamshedpur students, professors, staffs & guards to sell / Purchase any product, so that it helps all NITJSR family members.


To register in NITJSR Online Auction Site. You have only NITJSR email id. Once you registered, you can sell as well as you can purchase the items by selecting the  category and provide some necessary details.



If further you have any queries, feel free to contact us. You can contact us by writting your message in the below section .
Steps Follow to Purchase/Sell an items:-

i) Login into the system by User Login.
ii) It will show you the available products for bidding with their starting price and the bidding ends date with a link for bidding in each items.
iii) Once you click on bidding you will see the current bidding price of that product and also you can put your bidding amount.
iv) By clicking the Add Product on the menu bar you can can add products by selecting products and some necessary details about product.
V) On clicking the My bidding on the menu bar you can see the bidding on your products with details.
